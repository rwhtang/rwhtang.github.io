VanAir Design

Website Design

VanAir Design is a Vancouver-based design company originating from a capstone course at the University of British Colubmia after designing their signature VanAir Door - a ventilated door that allows air circulation between rooms without losing acoustic or visual privacy. After receiving tremendous interest, I worked as a team of four to conceptualize and design their offical website. I was primarily responsible for the project management aspects - gathering user requirements, conducting feasibility analyses, front-end development, and copywriting for the product page for their VanAir Door.
